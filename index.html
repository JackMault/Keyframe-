What this is

We build one intentional visual statement around who you are now and who you are stepping into. The goal is simple: create images that feel true, weighty, and unmistakably yours.

The Identity Session

A premium portrait experience designed for people who want more than nice photos. You walk away with a set of crafted images — including one signature portrait that provides sensory evidence of your evolution.

Selected work

The process

A transparent framework designed to remove uncertainty so you can simply show up.

Step 01

Clarify

We start with a conversation about what season you are in, what is changing, and how you want this work to feel.

Step 02

Create

The session is directed and intentional. You do not need to know how to pose or perform. I guide the entire experience.

Step 03

Receive

You receive a refined final set, centered around one defining image with real emotional and visual weight.

FAQ

Clarity on the logistics of the session.

Who is this for?

People in a meaningful season of change: founders, artists, parents, professionals, couples, or anyone who wants images that reflect who they are becoming.

Do I need to know how to pose?

No. Most people do not. Direction is part of the process.

How many sessions do you take on?

I keep these limited so the work stays intentional. A small number of sessions are accepted each month.

How do I inquire?

Use the inquiry button and send a short note about who you are, what season you are in, and why this work matters to you.

If this feels like the right time, reach out.

Identity Sessions are limited and built carefully. If you want portraits with actual meaning, start the conversation.
